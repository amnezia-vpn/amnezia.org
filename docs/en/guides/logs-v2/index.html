Working with logs

  • Русский Ru

Amnezia does not collect logs until you enable this functionality inside the application.

For this you should:

Click on the settings gear in the top right corner and then select “App Settings”.
In the Application Settings menu, check the box next to “Keep logs”. You can also clear logs and export from this menu.
To disable logging, click the checkbox again.
